Interest #1 Basketball

I've played basketball since the day I could pick up a ball. I played all four years of high school and went to the State Championship game four years in a row. We won 2x, one of those being my senior year when we went a perfect 27-0.

Interest #2 High Jump

I stumbled upon high jump by chance. I was dunking after basketball practice one day and my school's track & field coach just happened to be in the gym. He invited me to come perform in the meets even though I had no experience high jumping. I went on to take 2nd place in state my first year in the sport and ended up loving it.

Interest #3 Longboarding

Longboarding is one of my favorite forms of exercise. I love going to a beach and riding on the boardwalk. It also makes for a great date!
